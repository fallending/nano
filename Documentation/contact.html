Contact

Nano development is discussed on the libnano-devel mailing list; this is the best place to ask questions about how to use Nano, or ideas for future development.

You can subscribe to the mailing list here, and view the list archives here. If you have any problems accessing the mailing list, please contact me directly.

The latest release of Nano can always be found on the Nano home page.
